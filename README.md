# README.md

## Project Overview
This repository contains transport-related datasets integrated from two key sources: empirical data from the academic work of Lin Ma and Yang Yang, and urban transport data from the ChinaCitiesV1 platform. The datasets are intended for research, analysis, or application development related to transportation systems in China.

## Data Sources & Citations
### 1. Academic Paper Data (Lin Ma & Yang Yang)
The dataset is derived from the research presented in the paper by Lin Ma and Yang Yang. When using this portion of the data, please cite the original work as follows 
> "Lin Ma and Yang Tang. The Distributional Impacts of Transportation Networks in China. Journal of International Economics (2024): 103873."  

### 2. ChinaCitiesV1 Platform Data
Urban transport data is sourced from the ChinaCitiesV1 platform: http://chinacitiesv1.parisgeo.cnrs.fr/  
When referencing this dataset, please cite the platform as specified by its maintainers:
> ParisGeo CNRS. ChinaCitiesV1. Retrieved from http://chinacitiesv1.parisgeo.cnrs.fr/  

## Usage Notes
- Ensure compliance with the licensing terms of both data sources when using or distributing the datasets.
- For questions about the academic paper data, contact the original authors (Lin Ma & Yang Yang) or refer to the published paper.
- For inquiries about the ChinaCitiesV1 data, visit the official platform or contact its maintenance team.
